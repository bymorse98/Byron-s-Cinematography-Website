About Me

My name is Byron Morse and I have been working in video for over ten years. When I was a teen, I started a video production company called “Thumbs Up Productions“ where I grew up in the state of Vermont. I shot weddings, local sports, advertisements for small businesses in my community, plays and more. In 2018, I moved to Southern California to further my career in video and film production.

Since moving to the Los Angeles area I have worked on professional films, TV shows, commercials and music videos. Creating beautiful lighting setups and dynamically composed camera shots is what I strive to achieve in all my videos. While gear is important in creating a quality image, it is not nearly as important as the cinematography that utilizes creative visual storytelling to tell the narrative.
